Founder's corner

Jenisha and jero , the founders of JJ , is a new-age entrepreneur whose passion for creating nature-inspired, self care products has been a driving force of the brand.Their skincare journey began very early as she was an hostel student who was always out the sun. Natural ingredients helped her skin stay protected and healthy.

Growing up with sensitive skin, decided to steer clear away from products loaded with harsh chemicals. They maintained this resolve with every JJ creation as well. Jenisha and jero made sure every ingredient was handpicked and every product was responsibly crafted.JJ has now made waves and broke barriers as a community of Eco-conscious customers.
